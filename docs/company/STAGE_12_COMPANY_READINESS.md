OrderPilot must become understandable as a company, not only as a codebase.

This means we need:
- clear founder responsibilities;
- clear team roles;
- clear hiring order;
- clear CTO/cofounder requirements;
- clear investor data room;
- clear design partner pipeline;
- clear pilot offer;
- clear decision log.

## Non-negotiable product identity

OrderPilot is not:
- generic chatbot;
- simple OCR app;
- ERP replacement;
- accounting system;
- autonomous AI agent.

OrderPilot is:
- secure transaction intelligence layer for B2B distributors;
- AI-assisted but backend-validated;
- human-in-the-loop for risky actions;
- audit-ready and tenant-isolated.

- team roles are documented;
- CTO scorecard exists;
- investor narrative exists;
- data room checklist exists;
- design partner target list exists;
- customer interview script exists;
- pilot offer exists;
- 3-minute and 10-minute demo scripts exist.

# Team Roles and Hiring Order

## Required early team

| Role | Why needed | Priority |
|---|---|---:|
| Founder / CEO | Vision, customer discovery, investors, partnerships | 1 |
| CTO / Founding Backend Lead | Architecture, security, backend quality, technical hiring | 1 |
| Full-stack Engineer | Dashboard + API velocity | 2 |
| AI/Data Engineer | Extraction, evaluation, OCR/LLM pipeline | 3 |
| Product Designer | Operator cockpit, dashboard UX, human workflow | 4 |
| Solutions Engineer | 1C/ERP/Excel/customer onboarding | 5 |
| Customer Success / Implementation | Paid pilots and onboarding | 6 |
| Sales | Only after repeatable pain/message | 7 |

## Hiring principle

Do not hire “generic app builders” first.

OrderPilot needs people who understand:
- B2B workflows;
- backend correctness;
- data safety;
- integrations;
- operator UX;
- enterprise trust.

## First critical hire

The first critical hire is CTO or senior backend lead.

Reason:
OrderPilot is not a pure frontend SaaS. It is a transaction product with security, audit, tenant isolation, validation and integrations.

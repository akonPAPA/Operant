# Stage 6 Scope - Quote/Order Workspace and Exception Cockpit

Stage 6 creates the operator workspace for reviewing deterministic validation output and creating internal draft quote/order records.

In scope:

- Exception cases grouped from validation issues.
- Suggested fixes generated from validation and substitution data.
- Internal approval decisions.
- Internal draft quotes and draft orders.
- Operator notes and timeline actions.
- Audit events for important workspace actions.

Out of scope:

- ERP, 1C, accounting, warehouse, or other external writes.
- Inventory reservation or decrement.
- Customer email sending.
- Telegram or WhatsApp business replies.
- PDF quote generation.
- Autonomous AI approval.
- Production payment or invoice logic.
